About United Evangelical Church

The youth for christ fellowship operates under the United Evangelical Church, making it essential to consider the denomination's context and oversight when discussing the fellowship.

A brief history of the United Evangelical Church

The United Evangelical church (Founded as Qua iboe Church) is a Christian denomination in Nigeria. it has existed since 1887. it has more than 1,000 congregations and 500,000 members. The Church was founded by an Irish missionary, Rev Samuel Alexander Bill the first of many missionary who served with The Qua Iboe mission, now known as Mission Africa, which is based in belfast. The mission and Church were initially named after the place where the mission began its work, at Ibeno near Qua Iboe River in the present day Akwa Ibom State of Nigeria.
