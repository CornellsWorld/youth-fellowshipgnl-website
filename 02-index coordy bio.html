The Youth For Christ Fellowship

BACKGROUND

EJEH EMMANUEL GABRIEL is a devoted Christian leader with a strong passion for spiritual growth, youth development, and impactful ministry. Born and raised in Lokoja, Kogi State, he was nurtured in a Christian home where faith, discipline, and service were foundational values. His life reflects a deep commitment to walking in close fellowship with God and inspiring others to do the same.

EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

Emmanuel holds a Bachelor’s degree in Botany from Federal University Dutse. During his academic journey, he consistently balanced his studies with active ministry, demonstrating integrity, leadership, and a heart for service.

MINISTRY AND SERVICE

Currently serving as the Drama Coordinator of the church youth fellowship, Emmanuel is passionate about using drama as a tool to communicate biblical truths in creative and transformative ways. His ministry is inspired by renowned Christian dramatists such as Mike Bamiloye and The Winlos, whose works continue to influence his approach to evangelism and youth engagement.

PERSONAL LIFE

He is single and dedicated to serving the Lord with all his heart.

HOBBIES

Emmanuel enjoys activities that enrich both his personal and spiritual life, including: Reading literature that fosters intellectual and spiritual development Engaging in meaningful conversations that promote growth and mentorship Traveling to experience new cultures and broaden his worldview

DRIVE

Emmanuel is deeply committed to nurturing a generation of spiritually grounded and purpose-driven youths. His vision is to see young believers rooted in God’s Word, actively serving in their communities, and equipped to lead with boldness, integrity, and godly wisdom.
